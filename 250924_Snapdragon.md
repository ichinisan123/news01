# Snapdragon Summit 2025 Product Announcements Keynote - YouTube
250924

This video is a keynote presentation from the Snapdragon Summit, where Qualcomm announces its latest technological advancements and new products. The overarching theme is the new era of **AI-powered computing**, which is becoming deeply integrated into how we live and work across a multitude of devices like smartphones, PCs, wearables, and XR glasses.

Here's a detailed explanation of the key announcements and discussions from the video:

### Core Technology Upgrades: The Oryon CPU

A central announcement is the **third-generation Qualcomm Oryon CPU**, a custom-designed processor built for both smartphones and PC platforms.
*   **Design Philosophy**: The CPU was designed from a "blank slate" to optimize for real-world use cases rather than just synthetic benchmarks. It prioritizes efficiency to improve performance across various tasks while conserving power.
*   **Performance Gains**: This new generation offers **nearly 40% better performance** and consumes **43% less power** compared to the first generation Oryon CPU introduced in 2024.
*   **AI Integration**: It is specifically tailored for AI, working alongside the Hexagon NPU to execute tasks for AI agents and run AI models directly on the CPU for added speed and flexibility.

### New Flagship Smartphone Platform: Snapdragon 8 Elite Generation 5

This is presented as the premier announcement for smartphones, described as a platform designed for top-tier performance, efficiency, and next-level AI capabilities.

*   **World's Fastest Mobile CPU**: It features the Oryon Generation 3 CPU, with a prime core running at **4.6 GHz** and performance cores at 3.6 GHz, a first for smartphones. It also has 12 megabytes of cache per cluster for enhanced multitasking, gaming, and AI performance. Benchmarks show up to a 20% improvement in single-threaded tasks and 32% in web browsing compared to the previous generation.
*   **Upgraded Hexagon NPU**: The Neural Processing Unit (NPU) has been re-architected to deliver **40% better performance** and **50% better power efficiency**. This NPU powers features like real-time facial expression control in games, audio enhancement, and AI-powered photo and video editing.
*   **Gaming Enhancements**: The platform introduces **High-Performance Memory (HPM)**, a dedicated 18MB high-speed memory cache for the GPU, which reduces latency and frame drops for smoother, sustained gameplay. The GPU itself sees a 23% performance boost with 20% more efficiency. Qualcomm highlighted its long-standing partnership with Epic Games in pushing mobile gaming boundaries.
*   **Camera and Video Upgrades**: The platform features a **20-bit Spectra ISP**, a first for mobile, which improves detail in low-light photos and videos. It also introduces "Dragon Fusion," an AI-enhanced computational video pipeline that processes every frame for richer colors and deeper shadows. A new feature, developed in partnership with industry leaders, is on-device artifact removal to eliminate reflections from photos instantly.

### New PC Platforms: Snapdragon X2 Elite Series

Building on the launch of the Snapdragon X Elite, Qualcomm announced two new platforms for Windows PCs, promising a "generational leap" in performance and efficiency.

*   **Snapdragon X2 Elite**: This platform features a 12-core third-generation Oryon CPU, delivering up to **31% faster performance** while using **43% less power** than the first generation.
*   **Snapdragon X2 Elite Extreme Edition**: This is the top-tier offering, "unleashed for ultimate performance".
    *   **Performance Leaps**: Compared to the previous generation, it offers **50% faster CPU performance**, a 2x faster GPU, and a 70% faster NPU. It is the first Windows-compatible CPU to run at **5 GHz**.
    *   **Competitive Edge**: Qualcomm claims it delivers 44% faster single-threaded and 75% faster multi-threaded performance than competitors, while using significantly less power.
    *   **The World's Fastest NPU for Laptops**: The NPU is stated to be over **five times faster** than the competition, enabling powerful on-device AI experiences.
*   **Snapdragon Guardian Technology**: A new security and management feature introduced with the X2 Elite platform that allows for remote locating, locking, and wiping of PCs, even if they are offline or won't boot.

### The Future is Multi-Device and AI-Driven

Throughout the presentation, speakers emphasized a future where multiple devices—phones, PCs, watches, and XR glasses—work together seamlessly, orchestrated by AI.

*   **Device-to-Device Orchestration**: Qualcomm is developing technology for devices to collaborate intelligently, sharing information and AI queries across each other and the cloud. An example given is using voice commands on AR glasses to record a video that is automatically saved and processed on your phone.
*   **Google's Vision for Android**: Sameer Samat, President of Android Ecosystems at Google, discussed the shift from on-demand AI to a proactive model where a personal AI agent anticipates user needs. He highlighted the expansion of Android and the Gemini AI across watches, tablets, cars, and XR devices, all designed to work harmoniously.
*   **Samsung's Commitment**: Won-Joon Choi, President of Samsung Mobile, reinforced the partnership with Qualcomm, noting that the Snapdragon 8 Elite is at the heart of their AI experiences. Samsung aims to bring Galaxy AI to over 400 million devices.

Finally, Qualcomm teased another addition to its mobile lineup, the **Snapdragon 8 Generation 5**, designed to offer more choice in the flagship tier, with more details to be shared later.
